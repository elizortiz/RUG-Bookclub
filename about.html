What is the Read, Understand and Grow Bookclub?

RUG is a book club dedicated to reading the works of Black and minority ethnic authors.
RUG's aim is for people to keep educating themselves about racism and other social injustices.
It is a pledge to commit to the fight against racism.

How does it work?

The RUG meets virtually once per month to discuss that month's book and exchange opinions about the topic the book touches upon.
Head over to the 'Books' page to see what books the RUG community has already read, and head over to 'Home' to see the upcoming book and sign-up to be a RUG member.

Who can join ?

Anyone and everyone!
The RUG Bookclub always welcomes new members who are willing to question and discuss their beliefs in a safe community,and de-colonise their minds.

Why join?

The RUG Bookclub provides you with a platform to learn more about social injustices through the works of Black and ethnic minority authors.
It is a safe and open community, which encourages meaningful dialogues between people from different backgrounds and from different parts of the world.
What's more, RUG provides you with an accountability mechanism to ensure that you keep fighting social injustices even when it is no longer trendy on social media.
